Veris reduces costs by removing information redundancy

The Veris platform is a blockchain that enables all stakeholders to share information in a secure, immutable fashion - and avoid the expense associated with building duplicate systems.

Distributed ledger plus smart contracts

To allow unrelated healthcare entities to share data with trust. Smart contracts reduce and replace portions of this complex process.

Strong Governance

All base data is secured by the encryption which allows for blockchains to function. Any patient or revenue data is additionally secured via encryption of the smart contract.

Governance of the chain is directed by a non-profit, and upgrades voted on by users of the platform.

Up to $59b in expense savings per year

Users of the platform will take advantage of up to a 10% reduction of operating expenses. Fully adopted this would have amounted to $59 billion USD saved in 2015.
